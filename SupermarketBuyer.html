Supermarket Buyer APP

Supermarket Buyer is an app which aims to help young people between 18 and 35 years old in the major cities to get the goods delivery service from supermarkets at any time.

Type :  Individual Project in a UX Training Class
Time :   2016/03-2016/04

Background

Young people (18-35 years old) in China tend to go to supermarkets regularly. They usually buy some snacks, fruits, and other house and living things on weekends. However, there are many factors which cause inconvenience for them to shopping in supermarkets, such as the terrible weather, the traffic jam, and the heavy workload.

Therefore, they need delivery service which could help them to buy goods in supermarkets. As mobile apps can provide service regardless of time and place, a supermarket buyer app is the best solution.

Food Delivery APPs

As the food delivery apps are now very popular in China, I studied the three most popular apps to find out the characteristics of delivery service apps. These characteristics can help me to construct the basic information architecture of this app.

  • Clear Classification
  • Convenient Operation
  • Visible Delivery Status
  • Location-based Service

User Research

I conducted a user research among 20 young people from different backgrounds and then made the persona based on the information gathered from the user research.
