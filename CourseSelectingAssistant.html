Course Selecting Assistant

Course Selecting Assistant is an APP which aims at helping students to select courses and check the timetable under any circumstances and at any time.
It's a course project which required us to evaluate and redesign a certain system in our school. We chose to evaluate our course selecting system and develop an APP to provide more convenience to students.

Type :  Course Project (Group)
My Role :   Group Leader, UI/UX Design, Usability Evaluation, User Research
Timeline :   2015/11-2016/01

Usability Evaluation

We used two popular usability evaluation methods which are cognitive walkthrough and heuristic evaluation to find out the usability flaws in the old course selecting system.

User Research

We interviewed ten students from different grades and different majors. And we gathered the answers that are most representative.
